This guide presents **60 habits** that help you reduce your environmental footprint while promoting a healthy, active lifestyle, perfectly aligned with the Irela Aqua and Fitness philosophy.

---

## Active & Low-Carbon Commuting (1-12)

1. Walk or cycle for all errands within a 2km radius this month.  
2. Use the stairs instead of the lift/escalator for the next 30 days.  
3. Try one new public transport route instead of driving this week.  
4. Check car tire pressure monthly to optimize fuel efficiency.  
5. Organize a carpool with a coworker or friend once a week.  
6. Walk for 10 minutes before lunch to break up the workday.  
7. Bike to a social event instead of taking a car this month.  
8. Plan errands efficiently to combine trips and reduce total distance driven.  
9. Track weekly travel emissions (or distance) and aim to reduce it by 10%.  
10. Find a safe, local walking path and use it twice this week.  
11. Avoid doing cardio on gym machines; opt for walking, cycling, or outdoor exercise.  
12. Replace one short car trip per day with walking or cycling.

## Resourceful Consumption & Waste (13-28)

13. Consciously acclimatize to the outdoor temperature, avoiding excessive use of heating or AC.  
14. Avoid buying single-use plastic bottles, cans, or tins this month.  
15. Bring your own reusable food container when buying takeaway lunch twice this week.  
16. Stop using plastic produce bags when grocery shopping.  
17. Bring your own coffee cup every time you buy a hot drink.  
18. Repair one broken item (clothing, appliance) instead of replacing it this month.  
19. Buy loose vegetables (not pre-packaged) for all fresh produce purchases.  
20. Buy second-hand instead of new for your next three planned purchases.  
21. Refill one home cleaning product at a zero-waste store this month.  
22. Avoid fast fashion items for the next six months.  
23. Only use reusable bags for all shopping trips this year.  
24. Research and start composting food scraps this month.  
25. Switch from paper towels to reusable rags in the kitchen this week.  
26. Unplug appliances when not in use to eliminate "phantom power" loss.  
27. Cancel paper junk mail subscriptions to reduce waste.  
28. Use a reusable water bottle for daily hydration.

## Water, Energy, & Home Efficiency (29-41)

29. Recycle 100% of household glass, paper, and plastic waste this month.  
30. Share rarely used items (tools, equipment) with friends or family instead of buying new.  
31. Perform seasonal decluttering and re-homing of items to exchange, donate, and prevent excess consumption.  
32. Take showers 2 minutes shorter than usual daily.  
33. Install energy-saving LED bulbs in the most-used rooms this week.  
34. Wash all laundry in cold water for the next three months.  
35. Run the washing machine only when full.  
36. Dry clothes outside instead of using the dryer all week.  
37. Reduce reliance on heating or AC by one hour daily.  
38. Boil only the necessary amount of water each time.  
39. Check one toilet or tap for leaks and fix it immediately.  
40. Turn off the water while brushing your teeth.  
41. Use natural light instead of artificial light in main living spaces.
