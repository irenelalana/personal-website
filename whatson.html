Experience a journey to a holistic Wellness. Second workshop: 01/06/2025.

This project is about transformational half-day wellness workshops, designed for latin migrant women looking to achieve balance, renewal, and empowerment through holistic perspective.

This immersive experience will allow you to rotate through different workshops, each focusing on a key area of well-being, ensuring a comprehensive mind-body transformation.

This is a time to honor yourself—to step into a space of self-discovery, self-care, and connection, knowing that the collective energy of women coming together can be truly transformative.

What Awaits You on the 1st June:

  • Reconnect with your sensuality, sexuality, and most intimate connections. Move your body with love, awareness, and freedom.
  • Learn to respect and honor your cycles and natural energy, and activate your deepest core muscles.
  • A safe circle for migrant women led by top professionals in sexuality and pelvic health. Gain important concepts you can apply to your personal situation.
