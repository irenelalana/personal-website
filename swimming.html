ADULTS SWIMMING LESSONS

Specialized in all levels adults swimming lessons. It is never too late to learn new skills, doesn´t matter how much afraid of water you are, we work on your floating, breathing, propulsion, improving your control, technique and fitness level as main goals.

I use video recording as a tool to enhance your learning experience, providing personalized feedback to improve your technique. My lessons are designed to help you achieve your goals in a supportive and encouraging environment.

Lessons are run at Musgrave Park Pool, in West End, Brisbane.

  • 10 Weeks program.
  • 45 - 60min private sessions.
  • $650 - $825.
  • Budget option: sharing lessons.
  • Reschedule within 24h notice.
  • Discounts with local partners.
  • Preference in spots over casuals.
  • Follow ups, reports and analysys.
  • Casual options available (try first!).
