About Me

Welcome to Irela13Coach, your ultimate partner in achieving your fitness and health goals!

My name is Irene Lalana, and I am a Sport Sciencist and experienced personal trainer and swimming coach with a wealth of knowledge and expertise in the field of sports. With a background as a professional swimmer and rescuer, I bring a unique set of skills and experience to the table, including goal-driven mindset, responsibility, and organization. With over 20 years of experience as a coach, manager, and instructor in various sports, different levels, clubs and organizations, I have honed my ability to understand the exclusive needs and requirements of different clients and provide personalized training and support to help you achieve your goals.

Whether you're looking for guidance in losing weight, learn to swim, plan your routine, or simply improving your overall health and well-being, I am fully dedicated to your success.

Thank you for visiting my website and YouTube Channel, I look forward to start our fitness journey together soon!
