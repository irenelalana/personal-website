AQUAFITNESS

Aquafitness increases endurance and enhances fitness, reducing blood pressure, boost mobility & flexibility; improves balance, coordination and agility, mitigates stress and makes a fun environment.

Sessions are run at Musgrave Park Pool, in West End, Brisbane.
